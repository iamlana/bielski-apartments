Guesthouse combines high quality service with a casual-classic atmosphere of a guesthouse.

The property is located in a secured, privately accessed second level of a classically-renovated Gdansk apartment building only a few minutes away from all major Gdansk historical sites. Bielski Guesthouse has four rooms with two shared bathrooms allocated in a nicely furnished 3 level building. All four rooms may have bed arrangements that will suit your best purpose. Spacious common area on the first floor at the kitchen is a welcoming hangout spot. The property has been renovated to an exceptionally high standard and all rooms are furnished with extra comfortable and spacious beds. There is a small library and kid’s area with plenty of toys and board games located in the common area. Iron and ironing board are located in the wardrobe at the entrance.Bielski Guesthouse would be a perfect fit for couples, families with kids, solo adventurers, and business travelers. We welcome group reservations up to 15 guests. For group inquiries, kindly Contact Us. Each room is named after a city-member of Hanseatic league and has its own character.
